PHOTOGRAPH

Photo is life

Photography is the process of creating still or moving pictures by recording radiation on a sensitive medium like film or an electronic sensor. The products of photography are called negatives and photographs, the latter being developed from the negatives. Photography became popular in the middle 19th century with Daguerreotype, and later wet plate and dry plate methods were invented. Most photography in the 20th century was on photographic film, and most in the 21st uses digital cameras. A photograph is a small creation full of details, movement, and emotions of a person. It is a gift of science that is always close to heart because it contains memories of people near and far.
